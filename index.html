Game Features

Randomized Maze Generation

Every time you play, the maze is generated randomly, ensuring a unique and challenging experience with each game. No two mazes are ever the same!

Time-Based Challenges

Race against the clock to escape the maze. Each level presents a time limit, adding intensity and excitement as the player navigates the labyrinth.

Power-ups and Traps

Find power-ups to boost your speed or give you special abilities, but beware of traps that can slow you down or hinder your progress.
